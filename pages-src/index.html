Areas of interest you can see—and defend.

VoroGaze explores landmark-based regions for face-focused eye-tracking research. The prototype makes coordinate assumptions, fixation assignment and the resulting metrics visible before they become analysis decisions.

30-second walkthrough

From landmarks to fixation metrics

The clip is silent and uses the bundled Lisa1 conference fixture.

What the prototype shows

A visible analysis chain

01

Place landmarks

Define meaningful centres directly against the face image.

02

Partition space

Generate bounded Voronoi-style regions from those centres.

03

Assign fixations

Inspect unaggregated assignments before summarising metrics.
